Description
^^^^^^^^^^^

Berlin Database of Emotional Speech. A German database of emotional utterances spoken by actors recorded as a part of the DFG funded research project SE462/3-1 in 1997 and 1999. Recordings took place in the anechoic chamber of the Technical University Berlin, department of Technical Acoustics. It contains about 800 utterances from ten different actors expressing basic six emotions and neutral.
